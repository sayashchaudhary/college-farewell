ABOUT

Some things never really conclude, as they leave behind them a sterling trail, with myriad memories to be infinitely viewed, they are absolutely non-pareil. It's always hard to reach the end of something so winsome, something you've been a part of, since its very beginning. With tears in eyes, and hearts so heavy, the students of B.Tech 3rd year organise "SANDISHYA'19" to envisage the journey our seniormost batch has savoured these 4 years.
